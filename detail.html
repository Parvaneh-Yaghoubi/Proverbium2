An Anxious Heart Weighs a Man Down, But a Kind Word Cheers Him Up.

دل نگران، انسان را سنگین و افسرده می‌کند، اما یک کلام مهربان او را دلگرم می‌سازد

This proverb highlights the power of kindness and positive communication in counteracting the negative effects of anxiety and stress. It encourages people to be compassionate and supportive towards others, as even small acts of kindness can have a significant uplifting effect.
