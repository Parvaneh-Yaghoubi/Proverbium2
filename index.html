Welcome to Proverbium

Proverbium collects the world's wisdom, one saying at a time. These are short sayings packed with wisdom that Afghans use in their everyday conversations. They function much like proverbs in other cultures, offering guidance and insight into various life experiences.

Featured Proverbs

Even on a mountain, there is still a road.

Meaning: This proverb suggests that no matter how difficult or challenging a situation is, there is always a way to overcome it.

When the water reaches the upper lip, learn to swim.

Meaning: It implies that it's best to take action when a problem becomes serious. It emphasizes the importance of not waiting until the last minute to deal with a difficult situation.

Proverb of the Day

A Real Friend is One Who Takes the Hand of His Friend in Times of Distress and Helplessness.

This Afghan proverb captures the essence of true friendship during difficult times. It emphasizes that true friendship goes beyond good times and shared experiences. It highlights the importance of loyalty and support during challenging moments.
